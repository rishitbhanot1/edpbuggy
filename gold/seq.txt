↓
Buggy 1 exits parking → moves clockwise

↓
Buggy 1 reaches center gantry (G3)
→ Stops
→ <B1_WAIT_CENTER>

↓
Send S2n → START BUGGY 2

↓
Buggy 2 moves anticlockwise:
    Right gantry → continue
    Left gantry → LOOP COUNT++

↓
Repeat until:
    completedLoops == n

↓
Buggy 2:
    <B2_PARK_ARM>
    enters parking (LEFT bias)
    <B2_PARKED>

↓
Send R1 → RESUME BUGGY 1

↓
Buggy 1 continues clockwise:
    Right gantry → LOOP COUNT++

↓
Repeat until:
    completedLoops == n

↓
Buggy 1:
    <B1_PARK_ARM>
    enters parking (RIGHT bias)
    <B1_PARKED>

↓
END → Both Buggies Parked
